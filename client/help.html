Filling out the property form:

Property Nickname:

Give your property a unique nickname for easy reference.

Address:

Enter the full address of the rental property, including street, city, state, and zip code.

Purchase Price:

Enter the total purchase price of the property, including closing costs and fees.

Loan Down Payment:

Enter the amount of money you plan to put down as a down payment on the property loan.

Loan Length:

Enter the length of the loan term in years. Most loans have a term of 15 or 30 years.

Interest Rate:

Enter the annual interest rate for the property loan as a decimal (e.g., 4.5% as 4.5, not 0.045).

Rental Income:

Enter the total monthly rental income you expect to receive from the property.

Expenses:

Enter the total monthly expenses for the property, including property taxes, insurance, management fees, maintenance, utilities, vacancy, HOA fees, capital expenditures, and legal or accounting fees. Review your specific property and adjust calculations accordingly.
